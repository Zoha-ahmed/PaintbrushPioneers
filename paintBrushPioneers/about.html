About Us

We are the Hues, the team behind Paintbrush Pioneers. Using our different abilities and skill sets, we are coming together to bring a new way for students to learn about color. Every member in the Hues has a background in art and a passion for it as well. We want everyone to have access to a free education on art and the theory behind it. Since color runs the world, we want you to be in control. Please check out our other projects and keep up with our progress!
